The app will be a simple way for a bowling league to track a progressive jackpot. Bowlers in the league can buy tickets to enter the drawing. The default starting balance for each bowler is $50 dollars, which can be changed by clicking on the initialize balance button. All tickets cost $10 dollars, and the proceeds go into the jackpot. After at least three tickets are bought, one ticket is drawn at random. The owner of that ticket gets to roll the dice (yes, there's only one dice, to make it not so hard to win) once. If they get a strike, they win the entire pot. Otherwise, they win a fraction of the pot, and the remaining pot rolls over into the next drawing.

Please don't forget to log in before you enjoy the game! :)
